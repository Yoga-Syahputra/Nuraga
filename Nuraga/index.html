About

Nuraga is a multifaceted virtual assistant meticulously crafted to empower and support Indonesian youths in their journey towards a fulfilling and successful life. More than just an AI companion, Nuraga is your trusted guide on a holistic path to personal development, mental health and well-being, academic excellence, a balanced lifestyle, optimal health, and financial literacy.
